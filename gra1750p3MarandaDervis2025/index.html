Cajun Chicken and Shrimp Alfredo

Tender Deliecious bites of chicken and shrimp are tossed with pasta and colorful bell peppers in a creamy, slightly spicy sauce to create this Cajun Chicken and Shrimp Pasta.

Ingriedents

  • 1 pound of fettutine pasta
  • 1 pound of shrimp
  • 1 pound of chicken
  • 2-3 tablespoonscajun seasoning
  • 2 bell peppers assorted colors, chopped into ½ inch pieces, about 2 cups
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 cup of Heavy Cream
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Place your fettutine pasta in a boling pot of water with a pinch of salt
  2. while the pasta cooks add atable spoon of olive oil in a skillet
  3. Spread the shrimp in a single layer across the pan and let it cook for 1-2 minutes, until it begins to turn color.
  4. Turn the shrimp over and cook an additional minute, just until pink. Transfer the cooked shrimp from the pan to a medium-sized bowl and set aside.
  5. Add the remaining tablespoon of oil to the skillet and add the chicken. Sprinkle with a tablespoon of Cajun spices and stir to coat. Cook over medium-high heat for 3 minutes, stir, and continue cooking for a couple more minutes. Transfer the chicken to the bowl with the shrimp.
  6. Add the onion, garlic, and bell peppers to the hot skillet. Stir and cook for about 5 minutes, until the onions are soft. Add a small splash of water and scrape up most of the browned bits. Transfer the vegetables to the bowl with the meats.
  7. Scrape up any remaining browned bits on the bottom of the pan while the heavy cream mixture warms.
  8. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss well to coat.
  9. Add a splash of the reserved pasta water, only if the pasta looks dry.
  10. Add the shrimp, chicken, and vegetables to the pasta and toss again to mix throughout. Remove from the heat and serve hot. Sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley, if desired.
